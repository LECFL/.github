# LECFL Dev Team

We are the developers behind **[LeConnect](https://app.lecfl.com)** — the internal platform powering the Lubavitch Educational Center, South Florida's premier Jewish day school serving over 2,300 students from early childhood through rabbinical college.

LeConnect is our custom-built solution for student management, enrollment, family communication, attendance, and more — built by our team, for our community.

## What We Build

- **LeConnect** — the core school management platform at [app.lecfl.com](https://app.lecfl.com)
- Internal tools supporting LEC's schools, camps, and programs
- Integrations across enrollment, payments, attendance, and family portals

## About LEC

[Lubavitch Educational Center](https://lecfl.com) was founded in 1967 and has grown into a comprehensive Jewish day school system, educating students with warmth, joy, and a passion for Jewish values.
